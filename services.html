Focus Agric

The company’s core business operations will start with animal rearing to make Ghana meat sufficient. We intend to add other cash crops with time. The company’s core business operations constitute a research site for animal breeding. We also have a network of farmers supported by Focus to help increase the production of ruminants and create market for affordable meat in Africa. Goats and sheep are among the top 3 most popular meat eaten by most People in West Africa The company will be managed by experts and indigenous people will be employed to fill key positions as when need arises. Other potential crops: Maize, cocoa, mushroom, catfish, mudfish
